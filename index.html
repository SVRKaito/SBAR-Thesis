What is SBAR?

SBAR stands for Situation, Background, Assessment, and Recommendation. It is a structured method for nurses and healthcare providers to communicate critical information clearly and effectively, especially during shift handovers or emergency situations.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Improves patient safety through clear, concise communication
  • Helps standardize nursing reports during shift changes
  • Promotes accountability with digital nurse signatures
  • Easy-to-use dashboard for documentation and shift summaries
